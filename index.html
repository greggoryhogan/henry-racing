Henry Racing Team

Henry Racing is a dedicated and competitive team in the USAC Silver Crown Series, known for its commitment to performance, precision, and the heritage of American open-wheel racing. With a focus on consistency and craftsmanship, Henry Racing brings a hardworking, detail-driven approach to every event, competing on some of the most historic tracks in the country. Driven by passion for the sport and a relentless pursuit of excellence, the team represents both tradition and forward momentum in the Silver Crown ranks.
